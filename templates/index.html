How It Works

1

Setup Your Profile

The journey starts with simplicity. Just enter your core metrics—like weight, height, and activity level—once into your profile. CalorieFlow instantly gets to work, using efficent, scientifically verified methods to calculate your ideal calorie goal. .

2

Track Everything That Matters, Effortlessly

Tracking your health is designed to be quick and comprehensive. You can easily log meals and workouts using our search libraries, and the system instantly calculates your calories consumed and burned. Crucially, we also track holistic wellness: easily log your water intake to meet hydration goals, and record your sleep duration and quality to optimize your recovery. This complete logging system ensures all data affecting your energy and progress is captured.

3

Display Real-Time Progress & Summary

CalorieFlow transforms your daily logs into actionable data to guide your success. Every night, the system compiles a Daily Summary that clearly displays your total calories, net calorie balance, and macronutrients, alongside your total water consumption and sleep hours. By continuously referencing this real-time, comprehensive information, you maintain the clarity and motivation needed to make smart choices and flow effortlessly toward achieving your target weight and a healthier lifestyle.

Q&A: Addressing Common Questions

Q: What happens to my data?

A: Your data is stored securely in our database. We only use your information (age, weight, activity level) for one purpose: calculating your calorie goals and tracking your progress. We do not share or sell personalized tracking data.

Q: What if I can't find a food in your database?

A: No problem! While our database is extensive, you can easily **add custom foods, recipes, or even whole meals**. This ensures your logging is always 100% accurate, even when enjoying your unique home-cooked favorites.

Q: Why does CalorieFlow track water and sleep?

A: Because health is holistic! Hydration and good rest are just as vital as calories for energy and metabolism. We track these to give you a **complete flow** of wellness data, helping you optimize performance and mood, not just weight.

Q: What does it mean to "Let Your Calories Flow"?

A: It means tracking smarter, not harder! When you provide CalorieFlow with simple inputs, the system does the complex math and provides clear feedback, allowing your progress to be **predictable and smooth**—a true *flow* toward your goals, free of stress and guesswork.

Q: Can I connect CalorieFlow to my smartwatch or other fitness trackers (like Garmin or Apple Health)?

A: Not yet! Currently, CalorieFlow is a self-contained web application. For the most accurate tracking, all activity and meal logging is done directly within the CalorieFlow interface.

Q: How do I set my initial goals (e.g., target weight, maintenance)?

A: You set your goals during the **Quick Setup** when you first create your profile. You'll input your target weight and timeframe, and CalorieFlow handles the rest by calculating your daily calorie needs.

Q: How do I reset my password or change my email address?

A: You can easily update your account details in the **Settings** section of the app. You can easily access the settings from the Home Page
